Spellbinding Charmed Pastries

Description: pastries asf

Ingredients:
  • 1 batch of crescent moon-shaped pastry dough
  • 1 cup of mystical blueberry compote
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered star anise
  • Cosmic sugar glaze for decoration
  • Essence of enchanted dreams
Instructions:
  1. Roll out the crescent moon-shaped pastry dough on a surface sprinkled with powdered star anise.
  2. Fill each pastry with a spoonful of mystical blueberry compote, leaving space to seal the edges.
  3. Fold the pastry over to form crescent shapes, sealing the edges with a touch of enchanted water.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until the pastries turn a celestial golden hue.
  5. Once cooled, drizzle cosmic sugar glaze over the pastries and sprinkle with essence of enchanted dreams for an extra touch of enchantment.
